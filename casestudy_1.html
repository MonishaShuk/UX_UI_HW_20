Responsive Design for a non profit - I CARE, INC.

Project Description: Redesigning the website for the non-profit, I care ( Volunteer driver for Seniors). We fell in love with this organization's mission and felt they could use a website refresh.

My role: User research, UI/UX design, Prototype & Validation

Team: Monisha Shukla, Haley Hetrick & Sara Howard

Timeline: 3 Weeks

The Context

I Care is a nonprofit that provides free transportation for fixed/low-income seniors throughout DeKalb County to medical appointments and grocery stores. I Care provides this service with the help of volunteer drivers, who use their own vehicles and gas to transport clients, and who provide caring companionship in our door-to-door service.

The Problem

The current non-profit I CARE website does not make it clear to potential volunteers how to sign up or what specifications are needed for volunteering.

Solution

Develop a responsive web design for the non-profit I CARE that helps volunteers better understand how to sign up for driving shifts and clarifies what volunteering entails.

Impact

Looking to analyze the increase in number of volunteers that sign-up in the app to become a volunteer.

Lets take a look at the Original Site

We wanted to enhance the user experience for senior citizens by making the site more user-friendly, intuitive, and accessible. So for the website refresh we focused on Large fonts ,clear navigation, simplified layouts, and better color contrast.

Design Process

Empathize

So the first step of our research was to identify who are the users for this non profit.

We focused on creating a proto persona for a volunteer: Jenn

We wanted to know what motivated the user to become volunteers, what were the pain points and challenges faced by them when selecting an NGO / Volunteering, was the current website accessible to the target audience of seniors and the volunteers.

We started with User interview and Survey’s, to determine the common and basic pain points of volunteering.

Define

We were looking for non profit who help senior citizens get to there destination and see what other options are available to the seniors we’re trying to serve.
